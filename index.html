Bibimbap

a Korean rice dish topped with sautéed vegetables, chilli paste, and beef or other meat, with a raw or fried egg at the center of the bowl.

Ingredients:

Sauce-

  • 1tbsp brown sugar
  • 4tbsp gochujang paste
  • 2tsp soy sauce
  • 1tbsp sesame oil
  • 1tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 2tsp minced garlic
  • 1tsp minced ginger
  • 50mL water
  • 2tsp korean chili flakes
  • salt to taste

Toppings

  • 2 Shiitake mushrooms(thinly sliced)
  • 3/4 small carrot(thin strips)
  • 1/2 small zucchini(thin strips)
  • 20g alfalfa sprouts
  • pork belly(thinly sliced)
  • 200g spinach leaves
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 Cloves of chopped garlic
  • 1 Stem rosemary
  • Salted butter(for sautéing)

Rice-

  • Large serving bowl
  • Cooked rice(enough to fill the serving bowl)

Procedure:

Sauce-

  • Step 1- Place the cooked rice in the serving bowl and set aside
  • Step 2- Into a saucepan, on low heat, add the sesame oil
  • Step 3- Once the oil gets hot, add in the minced garlic and ginger
  • Step 4- Sear until the garlic is a light golden-brown, then add the gochujang paste, brown sugar, water and soy sauce
  • Step 5- Stir well and simmer until the sauce reaches a ketchup-like consistency
  • Step 6- Lastly, add the korean chilli flakes and roasted sesame seeds
  • Step 7- Turn off the flame and set the sauce aside

Toppings-

  • Step 1- Boil the spinach until it significantly reduces in volume and strain
  • Step 2- Sauté separately, the carrots, spinach, zucchini, shiitake mushrooms, and alfalfa sprouts with butter and salt, and place them into separate bowls and set them aside
  • Step 3- Into a pan, add 1tbsp of butter, chopped garlic, and rosemary, and crank up the heat to medium
  • Step 4- Once the butter is bubbling and the garlic is golden brown, add the pork belly
  • Step 5- Let it simmer on low heat for 5-7 minutes. Set aside.
  • Step 6- Into a pan at medium-high heat, add 1/2 tbsp butter
  • Step 7- Once the butter starts bubbling, crack the egg into the pan and let it cook for 1 1/2 minutes, until the egg is fried.

Plating-

  • Step 1- Bring in the serving bowl with the cooked rice
  • Step 2- Pour the prepared sauce all over the rice
  • Step 3- Indivisually place each of the toppings side-by-side around the center of the bowl in a circular fashion
  • Step 4- Place the fried egg right at the center of the bowl
  • Step 5- Sprinkle some roased sesame seeds onto the toppings.

Enjoy~
